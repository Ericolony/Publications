# Natural Evolution Strategies and Variational Monte Carlo

**Tianchen Zhao, Giuseppe Carleo, James Stokes, Shravan Veerapaneni**
*Machine Learning: Science and Technology, 2021*

## Summary

Introduces quantum natural evolution strategies as a geometric unification of quantum/classical black-box optimization algorithms, achieving competitive Max-Cut approximation ratios.

## Citation Identity

Cite for: natural evolution strategies for VMC, gradient-free optimization of neural quantum states, optimization methods for quantum many-body wavefunctions.
