Face Anti-Spoofing and Liveness Detection

Task Definition

Detecting presentation attacks (printed photos, replay videos, 3D masks) against face recognition systems, especially in remote/mobile scenarios with domain shift between training and deployment environments.

Related Terms

face liveness detection, presentation attack detection, face PAD, remote face authentication security, source-free face anti-spoofing adaptation
