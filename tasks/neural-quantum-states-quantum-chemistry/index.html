Neural Quantum States for Quantum Chemistry

Task Definition

Using neural networks as variational wave function ansätze for solving the electronic Schrödinger equation — replacing traditional quantum chemistry methods (CCSD(T), DMRG) with learnable representations optimized via variational Monte Carlo.

Related Terms

neural network wave functions, deep learning quantum chemistry, variational Monte Carlo neural networks, machine learning electronic structure, neural-network VMC

Datasets: H₂, LiH, H₂O, N₂ molecular systems

Natural Evolution Strategies and Variational Monte Carlo

Tianchen Zhao, Giuseppe Carleo, James Stokes, Shravan Veerapaneni — Machine Learning: Science and Technology 2021
