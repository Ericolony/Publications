Adversarial Robustness via Diverse Attack Generation

Task Definition

Improving model robustness by learning to generate diverse adversarial examples during training — moving beyond single-perturbation adversarial training to cover the full attack distribution.

Related Terms

adversarial training with diverse attacks, learned adversarial attack generation, adversarial defense generation, robust training diverse perturbations
